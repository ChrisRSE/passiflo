# Privacy Policy — Passiflo

**Last updated: 2026-03-25**

## Summary

Passiflo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

## What the extension does

Passiflo is a local QA tool. It captures screenshots and HTML snapshots of the active browser tab on demand, and uses them to generate a structured text prompt. The prompt is copied to your clipboard for you to paste manually into an AI assistant.

## Data collection

Passiflo collects **no data**. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is recorded
- No page content is transmitted to any server
- No analytics or telemetry are gathered
- No cookies are used

## Data storage

- Screenshots and HTML snapshots are held in memory only while the extension panel is open. Closing the panel clears them entirely.
- Setup inputs (check type and criteria text) are saved to browser `localStorage` on your local device only. This data never leaves your machine.

## Network activity

Passiflo makes **no network requests**. There is no backend, no API, and no third-party service. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

## Third-party services

Passiflo has no dependencies on any third-party libraries, CDNs, or services.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/ChrisRSE/passiflo](https://github.com/ChrisRSE/passiflo).
